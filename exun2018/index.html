Welcome to Insulosis

About

Insulosis is a small island off the coast of Russia just recently discovered by Levan Ivanov. It forms a micronation consisting of an ecosystem with species never observed by mankind ever before. Come explore new terrains.

Terrain

Insulosis has an abundance of vegetation which forms the Javagreens towards the South, a freezing climate in the Cryoniche towards the East. Towards the North-East, is the humid climates of Obnimi where thrive the Obnimi which are named after the place. Towards the South-West lies the Aridian area which has a very dry climate. The North-Western part of the island the place inhabited and urbanised by humans.

EcoSys

Insulosis has a numerous new species, and many still remain undiscovered. One of the newest species is the Bobblehead.

Tours

Insulosis is a wonderful place to travel and explore and is on its way to becoming the most popular tourist destinations with more and more people coming to see the unique flora and fauna here. There are plenty of tour guides available to give you a tour around the now inhabited part of the island.

Foreign Exchange

Insulosis has a well-established foreign exchange industry. Travel passports are available to Insulosis since it is emerging as a popular tourist destination.
